What is a Commodities?

 

Commodities are the raw materials available in the world, such as coffee, crude oil and sugar gold. There are two kinds of commodities: soft commodities and hard commodities. Soft commodities are plant-based, while hard commodities are mining based. These products are often traded through CFD contracts and are favored by experienced traders.

How does commodity trading work?

 

 

Trading goods through CFD contracts is the best way to do it today, because you don't have to actually own them. Price difference contract transaction is very simple. You only need to speculate whether the price of the commodity will rise or fall. If you think the price of the commodity you selected will rise, you will buy it; if you think the price will fall, you will sell it.
